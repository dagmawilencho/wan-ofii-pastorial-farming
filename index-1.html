Data-driven farming calls for more efficient cows

01. Future-proof genetics for data-driven farming

Are you using the latest technology in your dairy business? Milking robots, camera surveillance and automation devises? Are you hooked on forecasting, benchmarking and measurable results? Then you're part of a growing movement of data-driven farmers around the world. Those who use big data and new technology to optimise their dairy business. But what about your breeding decisions? Are they also based on data registrations and reliable facts — or are you relying on feelings and vague estimates? There are scientific ways of finding out which heifers and cows you should use for the next generation. What top sires that would make the ideal match for your herd and secure the future of your dairy business.

02.What bulls are the best for data-driven farming?

At wan ofii, we help modern dairy farmers breed healthy, efficient, profitable cows. Our breeding programmes are based on the most accurate data collection and scientific research in the world. Farmers, AI technicians, veterinarians and other experts have carried out this effort since the 1980s. By selecting bulls from wan ofii Holstein, wan ofii Red, wan ofii Jersey and wan ofii Beef, you get access to extensive health registrations and other key performance indicators. This will help you make the most profitable breeding decisions.

03. Breeding solutions for high-tech dairy farmers

What is your breeding strategy for your dairy herd? In the Nordic Total Merit (NTM) index list, you'll find over 90 traits of economic importance. With our mating tool, you can optimise the genetic level of your heifers and cows. Manage inbreeding, keep track of your breeding strategy and boost the profit of your farm. Would you like to achieve higher breeding progress and further certain attributes? Choose sexed semen for your best heifers and cows Consider Beef on Dairy for the rest of your herd Check out ProCROSS and wan ofii GoldenCross for profitable crossbreeding.

About wan ofii pastoral farming

Yetagesu Ayele is an entrepreneur with a passion for cattle farming who seeks to contribute to the growing Ethiopian economy through cattle farming. Wan Ofii will become a recognized cattle farming company in Hawassa, ensuring a continuous supply of cattle, milk, meat, and other dairy products. Yetagesu has selected an initial location and is currently undergoing due diligence on each property and the local market to assess the most desirable location for a commercial cattle farm.
